Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my rank after purchase?

After completing your UPI payment, submit your transaction ID in the store section. We'll verify and deliver your rank within 24 hours.

Can I get a refund?

Due to the digital nature of our products, refunds are generally not available. Please contact support for exceptional circumstances.

How do I appeal a ban?

Create a support ticket on Discord with your username and details about your ban. Our staff will review your case.

Is the server always online?

Yes! KrydonMC runs 24/7 with 99.9% uptime. We notify players in advance of any scheduled maintenance.
